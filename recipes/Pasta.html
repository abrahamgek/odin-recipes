Pasta Salad

Description

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

This pasta salad recipe will be right at home among other potluck and picnic favorites.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressign with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
